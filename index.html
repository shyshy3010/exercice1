All our cookies are 100% homemade, made the same day according to strict hygiene and sanitation regulations. Cookies stored in their original box will remain fresh and soft for 3-4 days. The best before date is indicated on each box delivered. Our cookies can easily be reheated in the oven at 180°C for 5 minutes. Each classic cookie weighs between 80 and 90g. COOKIE SHOTS weigh between 60 and 70g. The BIGGY weighs between 450 and 700g..
